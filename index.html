    • Rachel Kim | 김정민

    I am an undergraduate student in School of Computing at KAIST, set to begin my master's degree at the HCITech Lab (advisor: Sang-Ho Yoon) in Spring 2025. Currently, I am conducting research on ambient interfaces for extended reality.

    I enjoy bringing our imaginative concepts into tangible experiences through XR, envisioning a future where AR becomes a pervasive part of our everyday lives. To achieve this vision, my goal is to make interactions both natural and versatile by facilitating easier prototyping for XR designers and developers.

    Research Interests: HCI, XR  |  Context-Aware Systems, Gestural Interactions, Prototyping Tools, Haptics
